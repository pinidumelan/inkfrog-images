316 Stainless Boat Chain Stopper / Devil Claw – 12 to 32 mm
316 STAINLESS STEEL · DEVIL CLAW · FREE SHIPPING
A heavy 316 stainless chain stopper (devil claw) that locks your anchor and chain home and takes shock loads off the windlass. The hinged claw drops into a link and holds it fast, so the load goes to the deck, not the gearbox. Sized for chain from 12.5 mm up to 32 mm.
