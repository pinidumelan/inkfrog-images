316 Stainless Bow Anchor Roller – 8–10 mm Chain

This self-launching bow anchor roller is cast from a single piece of marine-grade 316 stainless steel and mirror-polished to a lifetime finish. The angled base lets your anchor drop and launch over the bow on its own, then guides it home and holds it captive under way, while the smooth stainless pulley wheel feeds chain and rode without snagging. This 8–10 mm model fits 8–10 mm chain and suits vessels around ~9–12 m / 30–40 ft.
